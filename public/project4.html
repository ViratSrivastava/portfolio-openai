Solar Panel Detection

Overview

Using advanced AI techniques to identify solar panels in urban and rural settings.

Tools and Technologies

  • Python
  • TensorFlow
  • Roboflow

Challenges and Solutions

Dealing with overlapping structures and low-resolution imagery was a significant challenge. Advanced data preprocessing and YOLOv8 training addressed this issue effectively.

Results

Achieved a 95% accuracy in solar panel detection over diverse datasets.
